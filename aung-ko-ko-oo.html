Mission Statement

To advance artificial intelligence research, cultivate local talent, and enable technological growth through open knowledge — with a special focus on low-resource Burmese language processing and inclusive AI development for the Myanmar community and broader Southeast Asian region.

Low-Resource Language AI

Driving research into NLP and language models for the Burmese language — an under-resourced language with complex script, morphology, and tonal features that challenge conventional AI systems.

Research Community Building

Establishing collaborative research networks, mentorship pipelines, and educational resources to develop the next generation of AI researchers from Myanmar and the broader region.

Technological Capacity Development

Bridging the gap between global AI advances and local application by adapting state-of-the-art methods to contexts with limited computational resources and infrastructure.

Open Science & Knowledge Sharing

Committed to publishing open-access research, releasing public datasets, and sharing tools that benefit the broader scientific community and practitioners in developing regions.
