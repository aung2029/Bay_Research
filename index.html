Intro

I'm a Computer Science student at University of the People, USA, with a deep focus on Artificial Intelligence and its real-world applications. I am the Founder and Director of the Burmese Artificial Intelligence Research Institute (BAIRI) — a research collective working to advance AI research and education in Myanmar (Burma).


My research interests span Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, and Robotics & Human-Robot Interaction. I am passionate about building AI systems that are accessible, impactful, and grounded in rigorous research — particularly in under-resourced linguistic and geographic contexts.
